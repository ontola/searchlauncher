SearchLauncher replaces browsing through your phone with fast, keyboard-centric search.

Features:
* Search apps, app shortcuts, contacts, settings, snippets, and custom actions
* Use it as your default launcher, a home screen widget, or an overlay
* Launch web searches through configurable shortcuts
* Keep frequently used apps close with favorites and usage-aware history
* Store and copy text snippets from the search UI
* Use calculator queries, phone numbers, emails, URLs, and smart actions
* Customize wallpapers, widgets, colors, and dark mode

Privacy notes:
* Web shortcuts and search suggestions are optional
* They may contact third-party search providers when enabled
* Crash reporting is opt-in only
